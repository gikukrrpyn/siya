1. About This Service

СіЯ ("Сервер і Я") is a community web application for the UkraineRP Roblox roleplay server. It allows players to view their in-game documents, licenses, faction roles, and receive server-related notifications via Telegram.

The application is operated by the UkraineRP community and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Roblox Corporation.

2. Eligibility

By using this service you confirm that you are a member of the UkraineRP Roblox community. You must comply with Roblox's own Terms of Use when using this application. If you are under the age of 13, please do not use this service.

3. Roblox OAuth 2.0 Authentication

This application uses Roblox OAuth 2.0 to verify your identity. When you choose to authenticate with Roblox, we request the following scopes:

  • openid — to verify that you have a Roblox account
  • profile — to read your public Roblox username, display name, and user ID

⚡ We never receive your Roblox password. Authentication is handled entirely by Roblox's secure servers. You can revoke our access at any time from your Roblox account settings under Connected Apps.

We do not request, store, or use any sensitive Roblox scopes such as your email address, Robux balance, inventory, or any write permissions.

4. Data We Store

After successful authentication we store the following in our Firebase database:

  • Your Roblox username and user ID (public data)
  • Your Telegram username (if you access the app via Telegram WebApp)
  • Your in-app passport ID code (generated locally)
  • Your avatar URL (fetched from Roblox's public thumbnails API)
  • In-game records such as fines that are issued by server staff

We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way.

5. Community Use Only

This service exists solely to support the UkraineRP roleplay community. All data displayed — including faction lists, licenses, and player roles — reflects in-game information maintained by server administrators.

You may not use this application to scrape, copy, or redistribute player data without explicit permission from the UkraineRP administration.
