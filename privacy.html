Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 1, 2026

1. Who We Are

СіЯ ("Сервер і Я") is a community-operated web application for the UkraineRP Roblox roleplay server. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

🔒 Short version: We only collect what's needed to show you your in-game passport and documents. We don't sell data, we don't track you across the web, and you can ask us to delete your data at any time.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Roblox password (never shared via OAuth)
  • Your email address
  • Your Robux balance or inventory
  • Payment or billing information of any kind
  • Location data or device identifiers beyond standard browser info
  • Cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your in-game passport, faction membership, and licenses within the СіЯ app
  • Link your Roblox identity to your Telegram for document sharing
  • Allow server administrators to attach in-game records (such as fines) to your profile

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics resale, or any commercial purpose.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database operated by Google LLC. Data is stored in European and US-based Google data centers with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit.

We retain your data as long as you are an active member of the community. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Google Firebase — our database infrastructure provider (bound by Google's DPA)
  • Roblox Corporation — only for the OAuth handshake; they do not receive your stored data
  • Server administrators within the UkraineRP community, who may view your linked Roblox username to manage in-game records

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (visible in the СіЯ app)
  • Request deletion of your passport record at any time
  • Revoke Roblox access in your Roblox account settings → Connected Apps

To request deletion, contact us via @UkraineRPSupport on Telegram with your Roblox username and we will remove your record within 7 days.

8. Third-Party Services

This app integrates with:

  • Roblox OAuth 2.0 — Roblox Privacy Policy
  • Telegram WebApp SDK — Telegram Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase — Firebase Privacy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the application evolves. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or data deletion requests: @UkraineRPSupport on Telegram or @UkraineRPOfficial community channel.
